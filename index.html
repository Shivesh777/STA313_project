Visualizing Global Renewable Energy Transition

Progress Towards a Sustainable Future

Climate change and global warming pose urgent threats to our planet. This interactive visualization highlights 12 key technologies, from solar to marine, alongside 23 specific ones like coal and natural gas. Explore data across 235 countries over 24 years, tracking our transition towards a greener future.

Dataset Coverage:

• 235 countries worldwide
• 12 group technologies including solar, wind, and marine energy
• 24-year timeline (2000-2023)
• Comprehensive data on electricity generation and installed capacity

Data Source: IRENA (International Renewable Energy Agency)
